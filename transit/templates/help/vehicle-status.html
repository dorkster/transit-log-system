Another feature of the transit system is to keep track of vehicle maintenance information, reported issues, and pre-trip reports.

The Vehicle Status page can be accessed via the main menu under Vehicles >> Vehicle Status.

Update the maintenance information with the mileage that the next oil change is due and when the inspection sticker needs to be renewed.
The oil change mileage should be the full mileage number.
File a new vehicle issue
The list of reported vehicle issues can be filtered by various criteria
Edit an issue or mark it as resolved
A list of every pre-trip is also available. If there were one or more issues filed in a pre-trip, they are indicated here with links to the specific vehicle issue.
